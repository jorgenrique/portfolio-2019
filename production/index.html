Work tasks

A Product Designer (PD) in Actimo is expected to follow the whole development process, from feature or improvement discovery to the final release to production of said features or improvements in the platform. This means a PD has to wear many hats.

Product Owner

A PD needs to understand the SAAS environment, as well as B2B dynamics - as our customers are big enterprise companies in the HR realm - to identify needs and pains that we might be able to solve, create the appropriate stories for it, and prioritize them.

UX/UI designer

User research, idea validation via wireframes and design of final mockups, in a parallel research and dialogues with users, stakeholders and the development team are tasks a PD faces in their day to day role.

Project Manager

Because we use some mix of SCRUM and KANBAN, the development team doesn’t work with Sprints. This means the PD has to have an overview of the development process and the accuracy of the development time plan, as well as releasing features in sync with CS and Marketing departments.
