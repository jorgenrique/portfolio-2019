Contribution

As the product lead, I developed a design system for all the interfaces we'd make. Each product had their main color, but the structure would be similar. This would ramp up development times, and would help the team replicate code and maximize resources.

This allowed us to develop 1 product and 2 MVPs in 6 months:

Beehaviour

Allows precise knowledge of the costumer's behaviour inside a building. From people counting to waiting time, real time queue management and costumer journey, offers exhaustive knowledge of several KPIs from an intuitive and customizable interface.
